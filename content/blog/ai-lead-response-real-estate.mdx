## How the AI Lead Response System Works

Here's the architecture of a real-world AI lead response system I built for a Mumbai-based real estate client:

### Step 1: Lead Capture (Instant)

Leads come in from multiple channels — website forms, WhatsApp messages, phone calls, and Zillow/Realtor.com inquiries. Each channel feeds into a single n8n webhook trigger.

### Step 2: AI Qualification (3-5 seconds)

GPT-4o-mini analyzes the lead data — name, property interest, budget range, timeline, and communication style. It assigns a lead score from 0-100 based on intent signals:

- **80-100:** Hot lead — immediate voice call + SMS
- **50-79:** Warm lead — personalized SMS response
- **Below 50:** Cold lead — nurture sequence

### Step 3: Automated Response (Under 50 seconds total)

For hot leads, the system triggers a **Twilio voice call** with an AI agent that:

- Introduces itself by the agent's name
- Confirms the property the lead is interested in
- Asks qualifying questions (timeline, budget, pre-approval status)
- Books an appointment directly into Google Calendar
- Sends a confirmation SMS with appointment details

For warm leads, it sends a personalized WhatsApp or SMS message with relevant property information and a booking link.

### Step 4: CRM Sync & Notifications

Everything syncs to Google Sheets (CRM), triggers a Slack notification to the agent, and logs the interaction for future reference.
